Privacy Policy & Terms of Service for FaithNear

Last updated: May 8, 2026
Your privacy matters to us. FaithNear is built by a fellow faith community member and we are committed to being transparent about how we handle your information.

1. Who we are

FaithNear ("we", "us", "our") is a free online directory that helps people find faith communities near them. Our website is located at faithnear.com.

FaithNear was created by an individual faith community member with the goal of helping people — especially young people — find a church, mosque, synagogue, or other faith community that meets their needs.

2. Information we collect

When you create an account

  • Your full name
  • Your email address
  • Your encrypted password (we never see or store your plain-text password)
  • The date you created your account

When you write a review

  • Your display name (shown publicly next to your review)
  • Your star rating and review text
  • The date and time of your review

When you claim a church listing

  • Your name, email, and role at the church
  • The church name you are claiming

When you use the app

  • Search queries you enter (city, zip code, church name)
  • Basic usage data via our hosting provider (Vercel)
We do not collect: payment information, phone numbers, home addresses, social media profiles, or any sensitive personal data beyond what is listed above.

3. How we use your information

  • To create and manage your FaithNear account
  • To display your reviews publicly on church listing pages
  • To process church listing claims and contact you about your claim
  • To improve the FaithNear directory and user experience
  • To send you account-related emails (verification, password reset)

We do not sell your personal information to any third party. We do not use your information for advertising targeting. FaithNear is an ad-free platform.

4. How your information is stored and protected

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a trusted database platform that is SOC 2 Type 2 compliant. This means your data meets enterprise-level security standards.

  • All passwords are encrypted using bcrypt hashing — we cannot see your password
  • All data is transmitted over HTTPS (encrypted in transit)
  • Database access is protected by Row Level Security policies
  • Your email address is never visible to other users

5. What information is public

The following information is publicly visible on FaithNear:

  • Your display name (shown next to your reviews)
  • Your star rating and review text
  • Church listing information submitted through the "Add a church" form

Your email address, password, and account details are never publicly visible.

6. Third-party services

FaithNear uses the following trusted third-party services:

  • Supabase — database and user authentication (supabase.com)
  • Vercel — website hosting and deployment (vercel.com)
  • Google Maps & Places API — map display and church location data (Google Privacy Policy applies)

Each of these providers has their own privacy policies and security standards. We encourage you to review them.

7. Cookies

FaithNear uses minimal cookies necessary for the app to function — specifically, session cookies that keep you logged in. We do not use advertising cookies or tracking cookies.

8. Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Withdraw consent at any time by deleting your account

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at the email address below.

9. Children's privacy

FaithNear is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of FaithNear after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

We're here to help. Reach out to us directly.
